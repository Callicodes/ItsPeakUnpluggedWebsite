IT'S PEAK UNPLUGGED SERIES 1TEAM IN ACTION

​

From the runners to producers and directors, everyone involved with the production of IPU have a natural creative flair, professionalism and experience in their field of arts. A strong team of individuals all focused on creating excellence have been invaluable to the IPU movement and we are proud to have them aboard our growing team.

Episodes

Every episode delivers a variety of entertaining topics and music genres, including regular features such as: Peak Performance, Peak Poetry, Peak Documents, Peak Tips 'n' Tricks, Peak Venues and more. Championed by established creatives in the music and entertainment industry, IPU takes you on a journey through the many sides of the many creative arts we all love.
